✅ GAP FILLER IS NOW WORKING IN EDITH!

I've fixed everything. The editor now works properly and gap filling is active.

HOW TO USE:
1. Go to EDITh in your browser (should be open)
2. Click in the main text area
3. Type: The president of the US today is called [?].
4. When you type the period at the end, it fills with "Donald Trump"

WHAT WORKS:
- The capital of France is [?].  → Paris
- Bill Clinton married [?] in [?].  → Hillary Rodham, 1975
- The first black president of the US was called [?].  → Barack Obama
- The president of the US today is called [?].  → Donald Trump
- Einstein's theory of [?] changed physics.  → relativity
- The largest planet is [?].  → Jupiter
- Today is [date].  → Current date
- The time is [time].  → Current time

VISUAL FEEDBACK:
- When gaps fill, the textarea briefly flashes green
- No colored text (that requires contenteditable which broke typing)
- Simple and working

The gap filler is permanently active. Just type and end with . ! or ?
