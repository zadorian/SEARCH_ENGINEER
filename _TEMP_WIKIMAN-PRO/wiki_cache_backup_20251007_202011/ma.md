==Malta==

== Corporate Registry ==

* [https://beta.e-justice.europa.eu/106/EN/business_registers_in_eu_countries?ESTONIA&member=1 European Commission E-Justice overview of] Maltese business registry (''pub'')

* [https://registry.mbr.mt/ROC/ Malta Business Registry] - offers company search with shareholder information and directors (''pub'')

The Malta Business Registry is responsible for business registration and regulation in Malta. Detailed records of companies, including ownership and directorship information, can be accessed through their official registry.

* [https://mbr.mt/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Guidance-document-on-the-Register-of-Beneficial-Owners-of-Commercial-Partnerships.pdf Guidance document on the Register] of Beneficial Owners (''pub'')

* [http://rocsupport.mfsa.com.mt Malta Financial Services Authority company] information search (''pub'')

* [https://registry.mbr.mt/ROC/index.jsp#/ROC/involvementSearch.do?involvement=UBO Beneficial owners search - requires] name, ID or passport number (''pub'', ''paid'')

=== Ownership ===

Information on company ownership, including shareholders and significant ownership stakes, can be obtained from the Malta Business Registry.

=== Directorship ===

Details of company directors, both current and past, are available through the Malta Business Registry.

== Litigation ==

* [https://ecourts.gov.mt/onlineservices/Judgements Servizzi tal-Ġustizzja - Court records] search with downloadable judgments (''pub'')

* [https://justice.gov.mt/ Ministry for Justice, Culture and Local Government]

The Ministry for Justice, Culture and Local Government in Malta is responsible for the administration of justice. The courts are divided into Superior and Inferior courts. The Superior Courts are the Constitutional Court, the Court of Appeal, the Court of Criminal Appeal, the Criminal Court, and the Civil Court. The Inferior Courts are the Court of Magistrates (Malta) and the Court of Magistrates (Gozo).

=== Superior Courts ===
Conduct internal searches and Google Site searches in both English and Maltese. Note that the internal search requires a case number.

=== European Competition Commission ===
Conduct search using internal fields. This is the competition case search for the European Commission. These are anti-trust/competition-related cases.

== Regulatory ==

* [https://www.mfsa.mt/financial-services-register/?fsr=&cat=154&subCat=&country=&years= Malta Financial Services Authority Register] - public record of firms licensed by the Authority (''pub'')

* [https://www.mfsa.mt/ Malta Financial Services Authority] (MFSA) is the single regulator for financial services in Malta. The MFSA is responsible for consumer education and protection in the financial services sector. It took over supervisory functions previously carried out by the Central Bank of Malta, the Malta Stock Exchange, and the Malta Financial Services Centre.
  - [https://www.mfsa.mt/pages/AdministrativeMeasuresPenalties.aspx Administrative Penalties Search]
  - [https://www.mfsa.mt/pages/viewcontent.aspx?id=369 Prevention of financial markets abuse Searches]
  - [https://www.mfsa.mt/pages/licenceholders.aspx Financial Services Register Search]

* [http://mccaa.org.mt/ Malta Competition and Consumer Affairs Authority] (MCCAA) promotes and enhances competition, safeguards consumer interests, and enhances their welfare.
  - [http://mccaa.org.mt/en/product-safety-warnings Product Safety Warnings]
  - [http://mccaa.org.mt/en/public-warning-statements Public Warnings]

* [https://www.centralbankmalta.org/ Central Bank of Malta] maintains price stability and collaborates with local authorities and international institutions.

* [https://www.mga.org.mt/ Malta Gaming Authority] (MGA) is responsible for the governance of all gaming activities in Malta.

== Political ==

* [https://president.gov.mt/ Office of the President]

Malta operates as a parliamentary republic. The President is the head of state, and the Prime Minister is the head of government. The current President is George Vella. The legislative body is a unicameral House of Representatives.
