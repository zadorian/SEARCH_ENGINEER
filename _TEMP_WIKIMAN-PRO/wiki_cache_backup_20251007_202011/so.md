==Corporate Registry== 
* [http://www.iros.go.kr Search companies listed on the] company register of the Supreme Court. (''pub'')
* [http://startbiz.go.kr/index.do Korean online government platform launched] in 2010 to simplify the process of starting a business in South Korea. It incorporates the business register, tax information system and social insurance information system. (''pub'', ''reg'')
* [http://www.bizinkorea.org/main.jsp BizInKorea provides information about Korean] companies for investment promotion based on KORCHAMBIZ business information, operated by the Korea Chamber of Commerce and Industry. (''pub'')
* [http://dart.fss.or.kr/dsab001/main.do Searches company disclosure documents from] the previous year held by Korea's DART corporate filings information system. (''pub'')
* [http://englishdart.fss.or.kr/dsbc001/main.do The Data Analysis, Retrieval and] Transfer System (DART) is the repository of all Korea's corporate filings, searchable by company name in English. (''pub'')
* [http://gwanbo.mois.go.kr/user/searc The Official Gazette of the] Republic of Korea. (''pub'')
* [https://s3.amazonaws.com/kraken-sta Saramin is a Korean job] listings site that also provides company profiles with legal representatives, addresses, sector, legal form, and other company information. (''pub'')
