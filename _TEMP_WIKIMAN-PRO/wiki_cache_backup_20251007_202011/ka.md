==Corporate Registry== 

* [http://egov.kz/cms/en E-government platform providing access to] certificates or documented changes of registered legal entities. Registration is mandatory and requires a national tax number. (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [https://kase.kz/en/ Kazakhstan Stock Exchange - provides] lists of issuers with basic company information, including market data and company details. (''pub'')

* [https://www.gosreestr.kz/ru/ Register of State Property -] database of companies with full or partial state ownership. (''pub'')

* [http://www.ispark.ru Manager search for the Corporate] Registry for Kazakh companies run by Interfax Russia. Basic information free, full profiles paid. (''paid'')

* [https://gr5.gosreestr.kz/p/ru/gr-search/search-objects Service provided by the Committee] for State Property and Privatization, Ministry of Finance. Returns addresses, full names and company type as well as business identification number. (''pub'')

==Litigation==

* [http://eaias.sud.kz/courtActs/index.xhtml Unified search tool for Kazakh] court cases on national, regional and district level. Allows search by case number, parties or date of hearing. (''pub'')

==Regulatory==

* [https://opi.dfo.kz/p/ru/dfo-search/opi-search Ministry of Finance portal providing] company status, incorporation date, and consolidated taxes paid for current and previous years. Offers BIN numbers for companies and taxpayer search. (''pub'')

* [https://kgd.gov.kz/ru/services/taxpayer_seaRCH State Revenue Committee website with] database on all taxpayers (natural and legal persons, sole traders). (''pub'')

* [https://dfo.kz/ Ministry of Finance - Depository] of financial statements. Makes financial statements from financial institutions, joint stock companies, mining companies and state-owned enterprises publicly available. (''pub'')

* [https://nationalbank.kz/?docid=3221 National Bank of Kazakhstan -] Search for liquidated companies. (''pub'')

==Asset Registries==

* [https://gosreestr.kazpatent.kz/ Committee of Intellectual Property Rights] - searchable database of inventions, utility models, industrial designs and trademarks. (''pub'', ''reg'')

==Further Public Records==

* [http://serj.ws/salyk SERJ/СЕРЖ - allows searching for] individuals by personal number (IIN number), includes date of birth and partial ID information. (''pub'')

* [https://id.occrp.org/databases/#kazakhstan OCCRP Investigative Dashboard - collection] of databases relevant to Kazakhstan.
