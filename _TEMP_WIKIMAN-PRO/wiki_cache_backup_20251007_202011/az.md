==Litigation==

* [https://courts.gov.az/courtCases Azerbaijan's justice portal for case] information from district, appeal, commercial and higher courts. Azerbaijan's justice portal provides basic, but up-to-date information (including names of case participants) on cases, hearings judgements from district, appeal, commercial and higher courts. There is no search function. However, the judgements of a specific court can be accessed by navigating to the court's homepage on the justice portal. (''pub'')
