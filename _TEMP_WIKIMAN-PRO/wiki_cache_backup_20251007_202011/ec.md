==Corporate Registry==

[https://appscvsmovil.supercias.gob.ec/PortalInfor/consultaPrincipal.zul?id=1 Superintendencia de Compañías Ecuador]

Search will give you results so you can check if a company is registered there. For some companies you can click through to the details page where you can find management, shareholders, annual accounts, financials and original documents.

This website DOES work (as of Feb 2022), but it's incredibly temperamental. 9 times out of 10 it will crash or fail to load. You just have to keep retrying. There are also unofficial databases, but they have less information and aren't necessarily up-to-date.

[https://appscvsgen.supercias.gob.ec/consultaCompanias/societario/busquedaCompanias.jsf Superintendencia de Compañías - Company Search]

Company directory. More information on click through: general information, administrators, legal acts, certificates, annual information

[http://181.198.3.73/portaldeinformacion/consulta_cia_param.zul Superintendencia de Compañías - Search for Active Companies]

Search by name to verify if a company is active.

[http://181.198.3.71/portal/cgi-bin Superintendent of Companies and Securities]

This is a government directory of business entities and securities with additional searches for individuals. The database is searchable by keywords or company names.

[http://200.93.225.83:8080 Superintendent of Companies - listed company search]

The Ecuadorian Superintendent of Companies provides in depth reports, statistics and rankings of companies operating in the country. This is the stock market company search.

[https://www.bolsadevaloresguayaquil.com/ Bolsa de Valores de Guayaquil]

Stock Exchange with list of issuers, original documents, announcements, resolutions and dividends

[https://www.bolsadequito.com/ Bolsa de Valores de Quito]

Stock Exchange with downloadable spreadsheet of all listed share issuers

[http://www.industrias.ec/prjCigWeb ANDA]

The national institute of statistics (INEC) provides an open data portal for Ecuador. The ANDA portal allows you to search for statistics of Ecuadorian companies. Chamber of Industry of Guayaquil Camara de Industrias des Guayaquil is an organization that represents and promotes the industry and commerce in the region of Guayaquil. The site has a Membership Directory to search Ecuadorian companies and businesses.

[http://www.edina.com.ec Ecuador Yellow Pages]

Ecuador Yellow Pages - people search.

[http://www.iadb.org Inter-American Development Bank - Approved Projects]

Database of projects of the Inter-American Development Bank. Supports search by keyword, project/operation number, country and includes more filter options (status, topic, sector, subsector etc). Retrieves information about country, project name, project number, amount of financing, and approval date. More information can be obtained by clicking on the project name (extra project documents).

[https://declaraciones.sri.gob.ec Internal Revenue Service of Ecuador Servicio de Rentas Internas del Ecuador]

Taxpayer search by company name. Searches that generate too many results will get an error page.

[https://www.camaraisrael.org.il Israel-Latin American Chamber of Commerce Cámara de Comercio Israel - América Latina]

Members index for the Israel-Latin American Chamber of Commerce. Search by member name.

[http://www.policiaecuador.gob.ec Registered Private Security Companies, Ecuador]

Searches a database of private security firms that are registered with the Ecuadorian police.
