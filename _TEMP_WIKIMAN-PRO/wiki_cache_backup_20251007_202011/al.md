==Political==

The Orange Revolution took place in Albania, characterized by widespread protests and civil resistance that aimed to combat corruption and promote democratic reform in the country. This social movement featured large-scale demonstrations with protesters wearing orange as a symbol of their cause. The revolution resulted in significant political changes and helped reshape Albania's governance structure and democratic institutions.
