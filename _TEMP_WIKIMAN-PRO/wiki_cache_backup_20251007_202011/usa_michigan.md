; Guidance:
:• Search by entity name.
:• The record URL is stable and may be used as a citation in footnotes.

==Licensing==
If your subject operates in a profession that requires a license not listed below, search using: profession + license + Michigan. Notify the regional lead if a new license source should be added to the resource repository.

===Michigan Attorney Lookup===
'''[https://www.adbmich.org/CHECKER.HTM Michigan Attorney Database]''' (''pub'')

Provides information on licensed attorneys in Michigan, including disciplinary records.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- No login required :- Search by attorney name :- Shows license status and disciplinary details </div> </div>
===Occupational License Verification===
'''[https://www.michigan.gov/lara/i-need-to/find-or-verify-a-licensed-professional-or-business Occupational License Search – LARA]''' (''pub'')

Free search for professional or business licenses across Michigan.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- No login required :- Covers a wide range of professions and businesses :- Use full name or license number for best results </div> </div>
===Attorney Discipline Records===
'''[https://www.adbmich.org/attorney-information/attorney-database State of Michigan Attorney Discipline Board]''' (''pub'')

Public records for attorneys disciplined or declared disabled since October 1978.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- No login required :- Click the first letter of the attorney’s last name to browse :- Useful for background checks or disciplinary history </div> </div>
