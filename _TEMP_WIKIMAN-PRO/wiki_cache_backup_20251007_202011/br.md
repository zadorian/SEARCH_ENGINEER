==Brazil==

==Corporate Registry==

* [http://www.jucea.am.gov.br/links-e-servicos/ Amazonas State Board of Trade] - Business Registration Information Access System (''pub'', ''reg''). Requires account creation with Brazilian citizenship.

* [http://www.b3.com.br/en_us/products-and-services/trading/equities/listed-companies.htm Brasil Bolsa Balcao] - List of companies on Brazil Stock Exchange (''pub''). Provides company classification, website, fines, finances, major shareholders, financial reports, corporate actions, and trading summaries.

* [http://servicos.receita.fazenda.gov.br/Servicos/cnpjreva/Cnpjreva_Solicitacao.asp?cnpj=09451084000154 Business Listing of Paraíba] - National business database (''pub''). Searchable by company number and provides company activity and directors information.

* [https://www.ccfacil.com.br/ CCFácil] - Company profile database (''paid'', ''reg''). Requires registered account. Provides financial information, debts, lawsuits, shareholders, corporate structure and activity.

* [http://receita.economia.gov.br/orientacao/tributaria/cadastros/consultas-cnpj CNPJ National Register of Legal] Entities (''pub'')

* [http://www.jucec.ce.gov.br/ Commercial Board of Ceará] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.jucema.ma.gov.br/ Commercial Board of Maranhão] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [https://www.jucemg.mg.gov.br/ibr/inicio Commercial Board of Minas Gerais] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.juntacomercial.pr.gov.br/ Commercial Board of Paraná] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.jucergs.rs.gov.br/ Commercial Board of Rio Grande] do Sul (''pub'', ''reg''). Requires account registration with valid national identity number.

* [http://www.jucisrs.rs.gov.br/ Commercial Board of Rio Grande] do Sul (updated URL) (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.jucap.ap.gov.br/ Commercial Registry of Amapá] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.juceb.ba.gov.br/servicos/ Commercial Registry of Bahia] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [https://www.jucees.es.gov.br/consultaempresasn/ Commercial Registry of Espírito Santo] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [https://portalservicos.jucemat.mt.gov.br/auth/realms/Portalservicos/protocol/openid-connect/auth?response_type=code&client_id=portalexterno&redirect_uri=http%3A%2F%2Fportalservicos.jucemat.mt.gov.br%2FPortal%2Fpages%2Fprincipal.jsf&state=163515%2F7f3e2f1e-1475-4d0e-9fdf-3816c3987d88&login=true Commercial Registry of Mato Grosso] du Sul (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://iged.jucepe.pe.gov.br:8081/ Commercial Registry of Pernambuco] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.ceciex.com.br/ Conselho Brasileiro das Empresas Comerciais] Importadoras e Exportadoras (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [https://www.jucerja.rj.gov.br/Servicos/BuscaEmpresasArtigo60 Governo do Rio de Janeiro] - Business search (''pub'')

* [http://www.institucional.jucesp.sp.gov.br/ Junta Comercial de São Paulo] (''pub'', ''reg''). Official registry for São Paulo state. Supports search by company name, type, date, address, and NIRE Number. Detailed information available with login.

* [https://esic.cgu.gov.br/sistema/site/index.aspx Junta Comercial do Distrito Federal] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.cge.go.gov.br/ouvidoria/Register_1.php Junta Comercial do Estado de] Goiás (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://jucetins.to.gov.br/ The Commercial Board of Tocantins] (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.juceal.al.gov.br Alagoas- Commercial Registry of Alagoas State] (''pub'', ''reg''). Company certificates can be requested using Brazilian personal identification (CPF).

* [http://www.facilita.al.gov.br/ Alagoas - Facilita Alagoas] (''pub'', ''reg''). Requires CPF and personal details.

* [http://sintegra.sefaz.al.gov.br/#/ Alagoas - Public Consultation to the Cadastre of the State of Alagoas] (''pub''). Search by CNPJ for registration data.

* [https://www.receita.fazenda.gov.br/PessoaJuridica/CNPJ/cnpjreva/Cnpjreva_Solicitacao.asp Receita Federal (Internal Revenue Service)] (''pub''). Main source of corporate records at federal level. Contains company name, incorporation date, registered address, stated activities and directors. Some companies (Limitada) must disclose shareholders, others (Sociedades Anonimas) are not required to.
