==Slovenia==

==Corporate Registry==

* [http://www.ajpes.si/Default.asp?language=english&mdres=1 Agency of the Republic of Slovenia for Public Legal Records and Related Services (AJPES)] (''pub'', ''reg'') - Official Slovenian company registry is free to access but registration is required. Search is available by the name or ID number of the company for all company types and it gives detailed and historical information. At AJPES, more information is available in Slovenian language compared to the English version. Options in the upper menu: eRTR - data on bank accounts of companies (open, closed) JOLP - annual reports of companies eOBJAVE - insolvency cases w/supporting documentation.

* [http://www-en.kdd.si/ Central Securities Clearing Corporation] (''pub'') - The list of issuers and securities available. Available in English.

* [https://beta.e-justice.europa.eu/106/EN/business_registers_in_eu_countries?SLOVENIA&member=1 European Commission E-Justice] (''pub'') - European Commission overview of the Slovenian business registry, including links to the registry, description of how to search and what can be found as well as legislation behind it.

* [http://www.gvin.com/ Gvin (Commercial business registry of Bisnode)] (''paid'', ''reg'') - Search by names of directors/shareholders. Offers all the financial data from annual reports (incl. for years before 2008/2009, which are usually the earliest years you get annual reports for at Ajpes). Info on tax payments, ongoing lawsuits and credit ratings (the latter is a payable service of Ajpes). If a company changed its name, Ajpes would return no results, while Gvin would detect it and put you through to the "new" company. Offers all available documents in one place (except those you can find only at the court register - memorandum/articles of association, insolvency reports, statute). You can click on the address of a company and see what other companies are registered there. Registration and payment required.

* [http://www.oam.si/default_en.aspx?doc=ISSUERS Info Storage] (''pub'') - Contains the list of issuers and the list of securities with company contact information.

* [http://www.ljse.si/cgi-bin/jve.cgi?doc=7026 Ljubljana Stock Exchange] (''pub'') - Search by company name with basic company information. Available in Slovenian.

* [https://e-justice.europa.eu/iri/int... EU Interconnected Insolvency Register (Slovenia)] (''pub'')

* [https://ebr.bundesanzeiger-datenser... European Business Register (Slovenia) -] Access to Slovenian corporate registry via EBR (''paid'', ''reg'')

* [http://www.ajpes.si/ The Slovenian Business Registry] - The name search on the Slovenian Business Registry website returns the registration number, business name and place of operation of a company. More detailed information, such as names of directors and shareholders, can be retrieved after creating a free account with the Registry.

==Litigation==

* [http://odlocitve.us-rs.si Slovenian Constitutional Court] - Website of the Constitutional Court of the Republic of Slovenia. This source searches all decisions and resolutions of the court published online.

* [http://www.sodnapraksa.si/ Judiciary Case Law Database] - Database of judgements maintained by the judiciary of Slovenia.

==Regulatory==

===Government===

* [https://erar.si/ Anti Corruption Commission's search engine Erar] (''pub'') - Erar shows how public money is spent. It contains information on all financial transactions of government bodies, public institutions and others which spend public money. It is free and no registration needed. Available in Slovenian.

* [http://seznami.gov.si/DURS/main_neplacniki.html Ministry of Finance] (''pub'') - Periodically publishes data on companies that owe tax. Available in Slovenian.

* [https://www.gov.si/drzavni-organi/organi-v-sestavi/urad-za-preprecevanje-pranja-denarja/ Office for Money Laundering Prevention] (''pub'') - Available: periodical reporting on bank and other transactions with companies in offshore jurisdictions (downloadable spreadsheets). The obliged entities referred must forward to the Office information on the following: - cash transactions exceeding 30.000 EUR (except auditing companies, independent auditors and legal and natural persons performing accounting and tax advisory services) - transactions or clients in connection with whom there exist reasons to suspect money laundering or terrorist financing. Lawyers, law firms and notaries are obliged to forward to the Office information on: transactions or clients in connection with whom there exist reasons to suspect money laundering or terrorist financing.

* [http://www.varstvo-konkurence.si/en/antitrust/decisions/ Slovenian Competition Protection Agency] - Official agency responsible for enforcing competition law and providing decisions related to antitrust cases in Slovenia.

* Securities Market Agency - Regulatory body that supervises the securities market in Slovenia.

* Insurance Supervision Agency - Agency responsible for supervision of insurance companies and insurance industry in Slovenia.

==Asset Registries==

===Land===

* [http://prostor3.gov.si/javni/login.jsp?jezik=sl Cadastre Office] (''pub'', ''reg'') - Useful when you have an address but not the cadastral number. Use this site to identify the particle number so that more detailed data can be obtained from the Land Register. Registration and digital certificate required.

* [https://evlozisce.sodisce.si/esodstvo/index.html Supreme Court Republic of Slovenia] (''pub'', ''reg'') - Land registry searchable by a particle number. Available in Slovenian.

* [http://www-en.kdd.si/ Central Securities Clearing Corporation] (''pub'')

* [http://www.uil-sipo.si/uil/dejavnosti/baze-podatkov/ Slovenian Intellectual Property Office (SIPO)] (''pub'') - Searchable database of patents and trademarks. Available in Slovenian.

* [https://register.epo.org/advancedSe... European Patent Organisation Register -] Detailed patent information (''pub'')
