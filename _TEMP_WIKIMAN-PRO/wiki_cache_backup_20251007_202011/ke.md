== Corporate Registry ==

=== Companies in Kenya ===
Kenyan companies are registered and maintained by the eCitizen Portal under the Business Registration Service.

[eCitizen Portal](https://www.ecitizen.go.ke/)  
The eCitizen portal provides access to various government services, including business registration. However, business registry name search requires the creation of a 'One Login' account, which is available to Kenyan citizens or foreign residents in Kenya (National ID or foreign certificate number required).

* [https://www.ecitizen.go.ke/ecitizen-services.html eCitizen- Business Name Search, Registrar] of Companies (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [https://brs.go.ke/companies-registry.php Business Registration Service - Companies] Registry (''pub'')

* [https://kenyachamber.co.ke/about-us/ Kenya Chamber of Commerce and] Industry (''pub'', ''reg'')

* [https://www.kenyachamber.or.ke/ Kenya National Chamber of Commerce] and Industry (''pub'')

* [https://www.nse.co.ke Nairobi Stock Exchange] (''pub'')

=== Ownership ===
Ownership information is available for companies listed in the registry. Reverse ownership searches are generally not available.

=== Directorship ===
Directorship information can be obtained through the eCitizen Portal. This includes details about the directors and their roles within the company.

== Litigation ==

=== Court System in Kenya ===
Kenya has a structured court system with various levels, including the Magistrate Courts, High Courts, and the Supreme Court. The Magistrate Courts handle first instance criminal and civil cases, while the High Courts handle appeals and constitutional matters. The Supreme Court is the highest court, handling significant appeals.

== Political ==

=== Political System ===
Kenya is a presidential representative democratic republic. The President of Kenya is both the head of state and head of government. The Parliament of Kenya is bicameral, consisting of the Senate and the National Assembly.
