==Barbados==

==Corporate Registry==

* [https://caipo.gov.bb/home/index.php/search/search-our-database Corporate Affairs and Intellectual Property] Office (CAIPO) - Ministry of International Business and International Transport. The website falls under the responsibility of the Ministry of Industry & International Business. Supports search by company name. Results contain name, number, date and category. (''pub'')

* [https://www.barbadoschamberofcommerce.com/category/members/ Barbados Chamber of Commerce &] Industry - Members' directory. Website of the Barbados Chamber of Commerce & Industry, includes a members' directory. (''pub'')

* [https://caipo.gov.bb/caiposearch Corporate Affairs and Intellectual Property Office Search] - The official corporate registry in Barbados, a common offshore jurisdiction.

* Barbados Stock Exchange (BSE)

Corporate Registration: Searches were conducted of the Barbados Corporate Affairs and Intellectual Property Office to identify corporate registration details.

==Litigation==

* [https://www.barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb/ Supreme Court of Barbados -] Court records filed and processed in the Court of Appeal and High Court. The Barbados Judicial System website includes court records, which are filed and processed in the court registries of the Court of Appeal and the High Court, respectively. (''pub'')

* [http://www.barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb Barbados Court of Appeal judgments] - Judgments of the Barbados court of appeal from 2012 onwards. Full judgment available in pdf on clickthrough.

* [http://www.barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb Barbados High Court judgments] - Judgments of the Barbados high court from 2012 onwards. Full judgment pdf is available on clickthrough.

* [http://www.caribbeancourtofjustice.org/judgments-proceedings/appellate-jurisdiction-judgments Caribbean Court of Justice appellate] jurisdiction judgments - Judicial institution of the Caribbean Community (CARICOM). The Caribbean Court of Justice was established in 2001. It is based in Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago. Appellate jurisdiction and original jurisdiction judgements are available as searchable PDFs. (''pub'')

Civil Litigation: Searches were conducted of the online databases of Court of Appeal judgments, High Court of Justice judgments, and Supreme Court judgments since 2000 to identify civil litigation matters. Online records in the Barbados may not be comprehensive.

==Regulatory==

The websites and/or online databases of selected regulatory agencies in Barbados were searched for sanctions, penalties, investigations, or other enforcement actions. Regulatory and law enforcement agencies often do not disclose to the public ongoing investigations or investigations that have not resulted in formal charges or penalties. The following regulatory websites and/or databases were searched as part of this public records inquiry: 

- Barbados Stock Exchange 
- Central Bank of Barbados 
- Fair Trading Commission 
- Financial Intelligence Unit 
- Financial Services Commission 
- Government of Barbados 
- International Business Division 
- Ministry of Finance & Economic Affairs 
- Ministry of Finance & Economic Affairs, Division of Economic Affairs 
- Parliament of Barbados
