[https://www.e-register.am/am/search E-Register Armenia] company search (Armenian): Official corporate registry of Armenia. Search by Armenian company name to retrieve status,founders names, registration number and tax ID. In order to view the full record of the company the user needs to register or sign in and then pay for viewing the respective record. 

[https://www.e-register.am/en/ E-Register Armenia] company search (English): Official corporate registry of Armenia. Search by English company name to retrieve status, foundersnames, registration number and tax ID. In order to view the full record of the company the user needs to register or sign in and then pay for viewing the respective record. 

[https://www.e-register.am/en/search E-Register Armenia] people search (Armenian): Official corporate registry of Armenia. Search by Armenian company name to retrieve status,founders names, registration number and tax ID. In order to view the full record of the company the user needs to register or sign in and then pay for viewing the respective record. Supports search by person's name, retrieves information about status, registration number and tax ID. In order to view the full record of the company the user needs to Register/Sign In and then pay for viewing the respective record. Only works in Armenian.
