==Corporate Registry==

* [http://www.caracasstock.com/esp/index.jsp Caracas Stock Change - Searchable] data by company symbol which yields up-to-date information on stock price, and recent company announcements (''pub'')
* [http://www.venexport.com/ Directory of Exporters - A] searchable directory of exporters in Venezuela yielding basic information including name, address, contact info, website, and products exported (''pub'')
* [https://www.fedecamaras.org.ve/ Federal Chamber of Commerce -] Government and daily notices/updates on commerce, policy, and trade are posted daily under 'Resumen de Noticias' (''pub'')
