==== New York Courts Overview ====

New York has 62 counties. The New York court system consists of the following courts:
* Supreme Courts
* County Courts
* Family Courts
* Surrogate’s Courts
* Civil Courts of the City of New York
* Criminal Courts of the City of New York
* District Courts / City Courts
* Town Courts and Village Courts
* Courts of Claim

==Licensing==
Start with the New York State Education Department’s Office of the Professions for professions such as CPAs, engineers, and physicians. If a license is not listed below, use Google to search profession + license + New York. Notify the regional lead if a new resource needs to be added.

===Professional License Verification (CPA, physician, engineer, etc.)===
'''[https://eservices.nysed.gov/professions/verification-search NYS Education Department – Office of the Professions]''' (''pub'')

Covers a wide range of licensed professionals (e.g., physicians, CPAs, engineers).

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- Enter name in format: `Last First` (no comma) :- Scroll to the bottom for “Professional Business Entity” to search for companies </div> </div>
===Real Estate (Broker, Salesperson)===
'''[https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/nydos/selSearchType.do NY DOS Occupational Licensing Management System]'''
'''[https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/nydos/nydoslogin.do?header=true Division of Licensing Services – DOS Login]''' (''pub'')

Used to verify licenses for real estate professionals.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- Search by name :- Covers brokers, salespersons, and firms </div> </div>
===Attorney License Verification===
'''[https://iapps.courts.state.ny.us/attorneyservices/redirect?0 NYS Unified Court System – Attorney Search]''' (''pub'')

Look up attorneys licensed in New York.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- No login required :- Searchable by name :- Provides license status and registration </div> </div>
===Physicians and Physician Assistants===
'''[https://www.health.ny.gov/professionals/doctors/conduct/license_lookup.htm NY Department of Health – Physician Lookup]''' (''pub'')

Search for licensed physicians and assistants, including conduct history.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- Use full or partial name :- Shows disciplinary actions, if any </div> </div>
===Skilled Trades / General Contractors (NYC)===
'''[https://a810-bisweb.nyc.gov/bisweb/LicenseTypeServlet?vlfirst=Y NYC Department of Buildings – License Lookups]''' (''pub'')

Includes safety managers, welders, plumbers, etc.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- Use the drop-down by trade type :- Returns license validity and expiration </div> </div>
===Business Licenses & Appearance Enhancements===
'''[https://www.businessexpress.ny.gov/app/index/sort/1,1 NY Business Express – License Search]''' (''pub'')

Covers barbers, pharmacists, beauty salons, and other small businesses.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- Enter business name or activity :- Filter by category for better results </div> </div>
===Notary, Home Inspectors, Appraisers, etc.===
'''[https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/lcns_public/main_screen NY DOS Licensing Services – Main Page]'''
• [https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/lcns_public/lic_name_search_frm Individual Search]
• [https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/lcns_public/bus_name_search_frm Business Search] (''pub'')

Used to search a broad list of licensed trades and roles.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- Use full name for individual search :- Use legal business name for entity lookup </div> </div>
===Food Service – Restaurant Inspections===
'''[https://a816-health.nyc.gov/ABCEatsRestaurants/#!/Search NYC Health Department – ABCEats Inspection Lookup]''' (''pub'')

Search by restaurant name for health inspection scores in NYC.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- Enter exact name for accurate match :- Ratings and violations displayed publicly </div> </div>
===Liquor License Lookup===
'''[https://sla.ny.gov/ NY State Liquor Authority (SLA)]''' (''pub'')

Covers liquor licenses for restaurants, stores, bars, etc.

<div class="toccolours mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">''Guidance'' <div class="mw-collapsible-content"> :- Search by license number, location, or business name :- Returns active/inactive status and license class </div> </div>
