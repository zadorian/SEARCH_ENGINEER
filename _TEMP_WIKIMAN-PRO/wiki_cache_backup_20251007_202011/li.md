==Lithuania==

==Corporate Registry==

[http://www.registrucentras.lt/jar/p/ Centre of Registers] (''pub'', ''paid'', ''reg'')

This is the official corporate registry. Basic company information and a list of all filed documents is available for free to anyone. Detailed company information is only available to Lithuanian citizens with a Lithuanian e-ID, residency or bank account. A simple extract (called a "master data statement in electronic form") costs â‚¬3.69 and lists directors, alongside other usual basic information, but it won't contain shareholders. There are also myriad other documents that can be bought separately for similarly small fees, including financial reports and the company statute. If there's one available called "shareholders' decision", that should list the shareholders (and their respective percentages) as of the date the decision was made. If there aren't any shareholders' decisions, there probably won't be any ownership information available.

Search by director or shareholder is not available due to legislation on protecting personal information. 

[https://rekvizitai.vz.lt/ Directory of Lithuanian companies]

This is a third-party registry that provides some more information for free than the official one, and it also allows anyone to buy specific documents (the same ones available in the official registry). Usefully, as each company page typically lists current directors (for free), this site provides the only way to search for a person's Lithuanian directorships. The site doesn't contain this function itself, but you can do a Google search with the person's name and site:rekvizitai.vz.lt. Of course, these types of searches shouldn't be considered definitive/exhaustive.

[https://beta.e-justice.europa.eu/106/EN/business_registers_in_eu_countries?LITHUANIA&amp;member=1 European Commission E-Justice] (''pub'')

European Commission overview of the Lithuanian business registry, including links to the registry, description of how to search and what can be found as well as legislation behind it.

[http://www.lursoft.lv A private corporate register run by the Latvian company Lursoft IT] (''paid'')

A private corporate register run by the Latvian company Lursoft IT in cooperation with the Lithuanian Register center provides access to the database of Lithuanian companies. Company profiles contain information on 100 percent shareholders only. Account registration is only possible with Baltic bank account or ID.

==Litigation==

[http://eteismai.lt/ Eteismai]

The unofficial source of court cases, searchable by case number, judge's name, court house, time period. The names of individual involved are not available because of personal information protection laws - all the names are diminished to their initials. The source includes litigation involving non-Lithuanian companies.

[https://www.teismai.lt/en Lithuanian National Courts Administration] (''pub'')

The official site of Lithuanian courts administration which includes three databases: reports about ongoing lawsuits, public searches of timetables and judgements.

[http://liteko.teismai.lt Liteko] (''pub'')

Manual search by person or company name is possible within Lithuanian District courts. Also searching "site:liteko.teismai.lt" + search term can deliver litigation results.

[http://www2.lat.lt Supreme Court of Lithuania] (''pub'')

Court orders from 1995 are searchable by keyword and offer court summaries.
