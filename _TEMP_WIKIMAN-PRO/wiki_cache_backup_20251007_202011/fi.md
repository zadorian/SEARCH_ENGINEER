==Corporate Registry==

'''[https://virre.prh.fi/novus/home?execution=e1s1 Patent and Registration Office - 'Virre']''' (''pub'')

Virre is a one-stop shop for data on legal entities. Log-in is required to access full information (see folders for username and password). This allows access to the historical information, register extracts (including English translation), financial statements and company announcements. Cost of each document varies but is usually under 5 euros.

'''Reverse directorship''' is possible in Virre, and it provides dates and position of current ''and'' former directorships.

'''Ownership''' information is not readily available. Such information ''may'' be found in financial statements available.

==Litigation==

'''[https://www.finlex.fi/fi/oikeus/ Finlex]''' (''pub'')

The Finlex database provides details on cases of the follow courts:

-- '''Supreme Court''' 

Contents:
Decisions published in the yearbook of the Supreme Court as complete text of decisions since the beginning of 1980, as well as indexes of decisions of the Supreme Court from 1926-1979.

Search methods:
folder search: you can search for a legal case from annual folders (according to the year of publication of the legal case)
direct search: you can search directly with the legal case number above the annual folders, e.g. KKO:2004:21
keyword search: you can search from the alphabetical list of keywords, e.g. with the term Free trial
application forms: quick search and advanced search

-- '''Supreme Administrative Court'''

Contents:
Summaries of decisions of the Supreme Administrative Court since 1944 and complete texts of decisions published in the yearbook since 1999. Short solution notes from 1946.

Quick search
Search from legal case text, e.g. home peace* and basic law* . *. Broad search, e.g. problem areas* or landfill* . Search by law, e.g. Section 191 of the Land Use and Construction Act . Search with a term in quotation marks, e.g. "children's interest" . Search by yearbook number, e.g. KHO:2002:23 ; solutions issued in 1987-1996, e.g. KKO:1983-II-50 . Decisions issued before 1987, e.g. KHO:1984-A-II-85 or KHO:1985-B-II-520 . Search word as Break character *.

Solutions without a yearbook number are searched for by selecting advanced search and then search by volume number.

Advanced Search
Search with reference information
Examples of diary numbers: 3316/3/02 , 2023/3/97 .
The date of issue is given in the form DD.MM.YYYY (e.g. 1.1.2003 ) or YYYY (e.g. 2003 ).
Subject word, e.g. car tax
Diary number, e.g.: 3316/3/02
Date of issue, e.g.: 7.7.2004
Volume, e.g.: 1653
Summary, e.g. severe disability*
Date of issue, e.g. 1.9.2004-30.11.2004 . Limitation to years e.g. 2002-2003 .

Search from text
In the text search, you can search simultaneously with e.g. a search word and a phrase. Search words are separated from each other with a space, the word break character *. The without words condition cannot be used alone, but must be combined with other search conditions.

With all words (and condition), e.g. parking fee* value added tax*
With any of the words (or condition), e.g. beach master plan* summer outfit*
With an exact phrase, e.g. good manners
Without words (no condition), e.g. nature preserve*

''The Supreme Administrative Court - other decisions''

Contents:
In the Other decisions group, individual decisions assessed as having social, regional or other general interest are published, which have not been published as yearbook reports or short decision reports based on the legal significance of the decision. There are other decisions from October 2013.

Quick search
The quick search on the legal practice page does not apply to other decisions. If you want the search to also focus on other decisions, perform the search as a refined search by also selecting other decisions in the search selection.

-- '''Court of Appeal'''

Contents:
The rights of appeal database contains legal cases of the rights of appeal since 1964. The legal case report contains a summary of the decision, legal force information and references to regulations, legal cases and literature. There are currently about 2,100 abbreviated judgments of Courts of Appeal, since June 2004 there are also long texts of judgments.

Quick search
The search targets the legal case text. Search from the text, e.g. children* and meeting* . Search with case number, e.g. I-SHO:2004:2 . *. Search by law, e.g. YSL ยง 2 . Search word as Break character *.

Advanced Search
The date of issue is given in the form DD.MM.YYYY (e.g. 1.1.2003 ) or YYYY (e.g. 2003 ).
Issue year, e.g. 2004
Court of Appeal, e.g. Turku Court of Appeal
Diary number, e.g.: H 04/760
Decision number (volume), e.g. 966
Subject word, e.g. violation of domestic peace
Case number, e.g. VaaHO:2003:5
Date of issue, e.g.: 01.06.2004 - 31.10. .2004 . Limitation to years e.g. 2002-2003.

-- '''Administrative right solutions'''

Contents:
Since 1979, the administrative law database contains decision summaries of administrative law and previous county law decisions, which briefly explain the legal issue, the facts, the decisions of the lower courts and the decision of the administrative law. There are also references to KHO's decisions and literature. The database also contains water court decisions from 1988-1998. There are currently around 2,100 administrative rights decision abbreviations.

Quick search
The search focuses on the legal case text. Search from the text, e.g. election* and sananvap* . Search by legal section, e.g. Act on the Publicity of Authorities' Activities Section 11 . Search word as Break character *.

Advanced Search
The date of issue is given in the form DD.MM.YYYY (e.g. 1.1.2003 ) or YYYY (e.g. 2003 ).
Year of issue, e.g. 2004
Court, e.g. Administrative Court of Turku
Diary number, e.g.: 02163/03/5300
Decision number (volume), e.g.: 03/0837/2
Subject word, e.g. environmental permit
Date of issue, e.g.: 01.06.2004 - 31.10.2004 . Limitation to years e.g. 2002-2003 .

Search from text
In the text search, you can search simultaneously with e.g. a search word and a phrase. Search words are separated from each other with a space, the word break character *. The without words condition cannot be used alone, but must be combined with other search conditions.

With all words (and-condition), e.g. environment* troublemakers*
With any of the words (or-condition), e.g. mental health* psychiatrist*
With an exact phrase, e.g. precautionary principle
Without words (non-condition), e.g. capital income*

-- '''Market Court''' 

- All Market Court decisions since 1979.

Contents:
Market law decisions and statements are stored in the market law database. The solutions concern three sets of cases: 1) Market law matters, 2) Anti-competitive matters 3) Matters concerning public procurement. The database also includes the entire ruling practice of the market court that preceded market law from 1979-2002.

Quick search
Search from solution text, e.g. language school* and hankin* . Search with solution number, e.g. MAO:118/03 . Search by keyword, e.g. product imitation . Limiting the search to antitrust issues, e.g. /KR and market position* . Limiting the search to market law matters, e.g. (/M1 or /M2 or M3) and imitation* . Limitation of the search to procurement matters, e.g. /JH and tender value* . Search word as Break character *.

-- '''Labour Court''' 

- All Labour Court decisions since 1970. New solutions are saved in the database at least once a month. 
Search methods:
1) folder search: you can search for a legal case from annual folders (according to the year of publication of the legal case)
2) direct search: you can search directly with the legal case number above the annual folders, e.g. TT:2003-13
3) keyword search: you can search from the alphabetical list of keywords, e.g. with the word Sickness pay
4) application forms: quick search and advanced search
