==Croatia==

==Corporate Registry==

===General Company Information (incl. ownership)===

The primary resource for accessing corporate records in Croatia is the [https://sudreg.pravosudje.hr/registar/f?p=150:1 Court Register] (''pub''), maintained by the Croatian Ministry of Justice. The register provides details on all registered companies, including their ownership, legal status, and historical filings. 

* **Searching the Register**:
  * Enter the company name or registration number ("MBS") in the search field.
  * For individual searches, enter the personal name.

* **Search Results**:
  * The search results include the company's registration number, date of incorporation, legal form, and status.
  * Ownership information may be available, but for full details, including shareholders, you may need to download the company's statute or other filings.
  
* **Obtaining Documents**:
  * The registry allows downloading official documents such as the company's statute, shareholder lists, and other filings. Some documents might require a fee.

===Chamber of Commerce Company Registry===

[http://www1.biznet.hr/HgkWeb/do/language?lang=en_GB Chamber of Commerce Registry] (''pub'')

The Chamber of Commerce website has a limited search of company information by number and by name. Results will show a graphical representation of basic information about the company, financial indicators and business analysis.

===Croatian Export Directory===

[https://www.hgk.hr/usluge/baza-uvoznika-i-izvoznika Croatian Export Directory] (''pub'')

This Croatian government portal provides information on relevant regulations and laws in the fields of investment and economics. You can search by name, primary activity, customs tariff (type of product), type of services. Information available for download includes contact information, primary business activities, size, ownership structure, and share capital. Additionally, you can search through a database of import and export companies.

===Directorship Information===

The Court Register also includes information on company directors and authorized representatives. Details typically include their full names and sometimes their positions within the company. Historical directorships can also be accessed through the same register.

==Litigation==

Croatia has several public resources for litigation records, although privacy concerns may limit the availability of certain details.

* **Croatian Court Register**:
  * [https://sudreg.pravosudje.hr/registar/f?p=150:1 Court Register]: Also provides access to basic litigation records for companies. It is useful to check for any ongoing or past legal proceedings involving a company.

* **Other Databases**:
  * Croatia lacks a centralized public database for litigation records that provide full case details. However, basic case information may sometimes be found in the Court Register, or through specific courts' websites by searching their decisions.

[https://sljeme.usud.hr/usud/praksaw.nsf/fSearchNew.xsp Constitutional Court Decision Search] (''pub'')

The Constitutional Court of the Republic of Croatia has online its records for decisions made since 2000. You can search by signature, case number, or a year and date. The search returns results containing all of the terms supplied, not just one.

[https://sudskapraksa.csp.vsrh.hr/search Supreme Court case law portal] (''pub'')

[https://sudreg.pravosudje.hr/registar/f?p=150:1 Croatian Commercial Courts Register] (''pub'')

This is the official website of the Croatian government court register of legal entities. This register allows you to search by name or keyword. You can either search all Croatian commercial courts or specify a particular county. Search results provides company profiles as well as status updates on proceedings before the courts.

==Regulatory==

Croatia has a number of regulatory bodies that maintain public registers relevant to financial services, business regulation, and sector-specific oversight.

===Finance===

* The primary financial regulator is the Croatian National Bank (Hrvatska narodna banka), which supervises banks and other financial institutions. They maintain a register of licensed entities, accessible via their [https://www.hnb.hr/en/home website].

[http://www.hanfa.hr/en/ Croatian Financial Services Supervisory Agency] (''pub'')

The Croatian Financial Services Supervisory Agency offers a variety of information that includes issuers list, participants list, corporate actions, ticker and ISIN, securities overview, statistics and frequently monthly reports, which contain data on business operations of pension funds, investment funds, investment firms, insurance companies, leasing companies, factoring companies and capital market.

[http://www.aztn.hr Croatian Competition Authority] (''pub'')

===Sector-specific Regulators===

* **Energy**: The Croatian Energy Regulatory Agency (HERA) oversees the energy sector. Their decisions and a list of licensed operators can be found on their [https://www.hera.hr/website].

* **Telecommunications**: The Croatian Regulatory Authority for Network Industries (HAKOM) regulates telecoms and postal services. Relevant databases are available on the [https://www.hakom.hr/en website].

==Asset Registries==

Croatia maintains various registries for tracking assets, including land and property.

* **Property**: The [https://oss.uredjenazemlja.hr/public/lrServices.jsp Land Registry] (Zemlji≈°ne knjige) provides access to property ownership information. Searches can be conducted by property address or parcel number.

* **Company Assets**: Company asset information may be retrieved from the Court Register, although detailed financial records may require additional resources.

[http://www.dziv.hr/en/the-croatian-intellectual-property-gazette/ State Intellectual Property Office of the Republic of Croatia (SIPO)] (''pub'')

The State Intellectual Property has an online searchable database in English. You can search for patents, trademarks and industrial designs in both national and international registrations. Available on this there is the official link to the Croatian Intellectual Property Gazette with the overview from 1994 till 2019.

[http://report.crs.hr/hrbwebreports/ Croatian Ship Registry] (''pub'')

The Croatian Ship Registry has information about shipping available. You can get detailed information by searching the name of the ship, IMO number or CRS No. The website is available both in Croatian and English.

[https://oss.uredjenazemlja.hr/public-services/review-lr-bdc Property ownership verification through Ministry of Justice] (''pub'')

[https://www.katastar.hr/ Central cadastre site] (''pub'')

[https://eplovilo.pomorstvo.hr/#/public/dashboard Ship register through ePlovilo portal] (''pub'')

[https://www.dziv.hr/hr/e-usluge/e-registri/zig/ Trademark register] (''pub'')

==Further Public Records==

===Insolvency===

* **Insolvency Register**: The Croatian Insolvency Register is part of the Court Register. It records all bankruptcy and insolvency proceedings against companies and individuals. Access it via the [https://sudreg.pravosudje.hr/registar/f?p=150:1 Court Register].

[https://id.occrp.org/databases/#croatia OCCRP ID database for Croatia] (''pub'')

[https://narodne-novine.nn.hr/search.aspx Croatian National Gazette] (''pub'')

The Croatian National Gazette website can be searched by number, publisher, publication date or which edition it was. The search includes the official, international and advertising parts of the gazette.

[https://eojn.nn.hr Oglasnik - Croatian electronic public procurement website] (''pub'')

==Political==

===Political===

* **Declarations**: Public officials in Croatia are required to submit asset declarations, which can be accessed through the [https://www.sukobinteresa.hr/hr/registar-duznosnika?contact_name=&field_naziv_duznosti_value=&field_tip_duznosti_value_i18n=primarna&contact_name_1=&field_active_value_i18n=1 Register of Interests].

With this website you can find Croatian public officials and find out whether they are politically exposed persons. You can search by the name of individual, his/her job position or year of data, whether the individual is still active in that political position and the name of the legal entity where the individual works.

[https://www.sukobinteresa.hr Conflict of Interest Commission] (''pub'')
