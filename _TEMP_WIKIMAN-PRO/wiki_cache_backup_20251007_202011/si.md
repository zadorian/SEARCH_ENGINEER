==Corporate Registry== 

* [https://www.tis.bizfile.gov.sg BizFile is an online business] services system offering registration services and searching of the directory of registered entities in Singapore. The service is operated by the Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority (ACRA). (''pub'', ''paid'')

* [https://www.bizfile.gov.sg/ Bizfile is an online directory] for corporate paperwork in Singapore. The site allows a search by company name to obtain basic company information, and a search by an individual's name or ID. (''pub'', ''reg'')

* "Small companies" under Section 205C of Singapore's Companies Act (annual revenue below SGD 10 million / approx. USD 7.5 million, assets below SGD 10 million, or fewer than 50 employees) are not obligated to disclose detailed financial reports

* Financial reports and annial reports are not included in the "Business Profile" of bizfile.gov.sg but can be purchased seperately for abut 20 dollars at

* [https://mytax.iras.gov.sg/ESVWeb/default.aspx?target=GSTListingSearch The Inland Revenue Authority of] Singapore (IRAS) is responsible for administering taxation in Singapore. The registered business search allows users to search businesses in Singapore registered for the Goods and Services Tax. (''pub'')

* [https://www.uen.gov.sg/ueninternet/ This source searches for entities] registered with a Singapore Unique Entity Number. The search returns the entity's name, UEN, registered address and registered status. (''pub'')

==Litigation==

* [http://www.commonlii.org/sg/cases/SGHC/ Supreme Court of Singapore] Database contains selected decisions published since 2000. (''pub'')

* [https://www.judiciary.gov.sg/judgments/judgments-case-summaries The Singaporean Judiciary] website publishes all written judgements by the Supreme Court since 2000. (''pub'')

* [https://www.lawnet.sg/lawnet/web/lawnet/free-resources Singapore Academy of Law's LawNet] system publishes judgements for the past three months from the Supreme Court, State Courts and Family Courts. (''pub'')

* [https://www.acra.gov.sg/prosecution-highlights/prosecution-of-cases-in-the-state-courts State Court] An additional selection of State Court judgements, primarily notable due to corporate applications, is presented on the Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority website. (''pub'')

* [https://www.lawnet.com/openlaw/singapore/judgments/supreme-court LawNet's OpenLaw platform] provides access to Singapore Supreme Court judgments. (''pub'')


* [https://www.lawnet.sg/lawnet/web/lawnet/pay-per-use-services '''Full Access to OpenLaw'''] may be purchased for SGD 58.06 per 30 minutes. Full access includes the following records:

*Supreme Court: ''all'' judgements issued since 1991;
*High Court: selected judgements issued publicly since 2000, or covered in the Singapore Academy of Law's Singapore Law Reports since 1965;
*Singapore International Commercial Court: ''all'' judgements issued since 2015;
*Court of Appeal: selected judgements issued publicly since 2000, or covered in the Singapore Law Reports since 1965;
*State Courts: '''all''' judgements issued since 2001 ''(as opposed to the past 3 months)'' ;
*Family Courts: ''all'' judgements issued since 1990;
*Selected cases from regulatory courts:
*Personal Data Protection Commission
*Intellectual Property Office of Singapore
*Various tax authorities

==Regulatory==

* [https://www.charities.gov.sg/Pages/ The Commissioner of Charities is] an office under the Singapore Ministry of Community Development, Youth and Sports. The site allows searching for registered charities by name. (''pub'')

* [https://www.mas.gov.sg/ The Monetary Authority of Singapore,] which is the central bank and integrated financial sector regulator. (''pub'')

* [http://www.cccs.gov.sg/ The Competition and Consumer Commission] of Singapore, which is the country's competition regulator. (''pub'')

* [http://www.cpib.gov.sg/ The Corrupt Practices Investigation Bureau,] a government agency under the Prime Minister's Office with the mandate to investigate corruption in the public and private sectors. (''pub'')

* [http://www.agc.gov.sg/ The Attorney General's Chambers, which] publishes the identities of those involved in major crime. (''pub'')

* [https://eservices.mlaw.gov.sg/ipto/forms/io/io-home.form The Ministry of Law's Insolvency] Office provides a bankruptcy search function. (''pub'', ''reg'', ''paid'')

* [https://www.sgx.com/ Singapore Stock Exchange]  (''pub'')

* [https://www.acra.gov.sg/enforcement-notices The Accounting and Corporate Regulatory] Authority provides search functions against disqualified directors and struck-off entities. (''pub'')
